Why Housing Trust Silicon Valley ?

Cities have homes set aside for low to moderate-income earners. We manage the purchase of these homes and provide loans to low and moderate income homebuyers in Silicon Valley in the form of low-interest second mortgages and down payment assistance.

group

What is BMP Program ?

California cities establish home purchase programs, often called Below Market Purchase (BMP) programs to facilitate the inclusionary housing requirements mandating housing developers to make a percentage of housing units in new residential developments available to low- and moderate-income households.

Mission

"Our mission is to make Silicon Valley a more affordable place to live. The Housing Trust makes loans and grants to increase the supply of affordable housing, assist first-time homebuyers, prevent homelessness and stabilize neighborhoods."
